German Pancakes

Description

German pancakes as the name implies, are very popular with German and Austrian. The center of a German Pancakes is supposed to be flat

aside the fact of it containing enough calories and vitamins, it contains fibres which aids digestion..

Ingredients

  • potato flour
  • milk
  • sugar
  • egg
  • oil
  • butter

Steps

    Put your potato flour, sugar, egg, milk together in a bowl and add water.
    Mix till it becomes paste
    Put your frying pan on fire and add vegatable oil
    When the oil is hot enough, take your flour paste and put them in the desired size
